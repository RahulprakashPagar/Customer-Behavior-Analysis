# Customer Behaviour Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer Behaviour Analysis is an end-to-end data analytics project focused on understanding customer shopping behaviour using Python, PostgreSQL, SQL, and Power BI.

The project analyzes 3,900 retail transactions to uncover:
- Customer spending patterns
- Subscription behaviour
- Product preferences
- Shipping trends
- Customer segments
- Revenue-driving demographics

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making and customer-focused business growth.

# 📊 Dataset Summary

The dataset contains:
- **3,900 customer transactions**
- **18 features/columns**

### Key Features
- Age
- Gender
- Location
- Subscription Status
- Item Purchased
- Product Category
- Purchase Amount
- Shipping Type
- Review Rating
- Discount Applied
- Frequency of Purchases

The dataset includes customer demographics, shopping behaviour, product details, purchase history, and subscription information.

# 🧠 SQL Business Analysis

Business-focused SQL queries were written in PostgreSQL to solve real-world analytical problems.

### Key Business Questions Solved

### 💰 Revenue by Gender
Compared total revenue contribution between male and female customers.

### 🛍️ High-Spending Discount Users
Identified customers using discounts while maintaining high purchase amounts.

### ⭐ Top Rated Products
Analyzed products with the highest average review ratings.

### 🚚 Shipping Type Comparison
Compared average purchase amount between Standard and Express shipping users.

### 📈 Subscriber vs Non-Subscriber Analysis
Measured:
- Average spend
- Total revenue contribution
- Subscription impact on customer value

### 🎯 Discount-Dependent Products
Identified products most dependent on discounts for purchases.

### 👥 Customer Segmentation
Segmented customers into:
- New Customers
- Returning Customers
- Loyal Customers

### 🏆 Top Products by Category
Identified the most purchased products across categories.

### 🔁 Repeat Buyer Subscription Analysis
Analyzed whether repeat buyers are more likely to subscribe.

### 📊 Revenue by Age Group
Calculated revenue contribution across customer age groups.

# 🔑 Key Insights

### 💳 Subscribers Generate Higher Revenue
Subscribers spend significantly more compared to non-subscribers.

### 🚀 Express Shipping Users Are High-Value Customers
Customers choosing express shipping have higher average purchase amounts.

### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 26–35 Age Group Drives Maximum Revenue
This age group contributes the highest revenue and represents the most valuable customer segment.

### 🏅 Loyal Customers Drive Long-Term Growth
Repeat customers contribute significantly to customer lifetime value.
